Women in Skilled Trades Outreach

As a female electromechanic with three years of experience, I've had the opportunity to challenge stereotypes and make my mark in a traditionally male-dominated field. From diagnosing and repairing complex machinery to maintaining electrical systems, my journey has been both rewarding and empowering. The hands-on nature of my work has not only honed my technical skills but also built my confidence in solving problems under pressure. Every day presents a new challenge, and each solution is a step forward in my career.
Through my work, I’ve become passionate about encouraging more women to enter this field. I believe that diversity in the workforce is crucial for innovation, and that’s why I am committed to outreach efforts that inspire young women to consider electromechanics as a viable career path.
In my outreach presentations, I share my personal experiences, demonstrate the versatility of the electromechanical profession, and provide insights into the day-to-day tasks of an electromechanic. I also highlight the opportunities for growth, advancement, and the potential to make a real impact on industries ranging from manufacturing to renewable energy.
This presentation is designed to empower and inform, breaking down barriers and creating a space where future generations of women feel encouraged to pursue careers in electromechanics and other technical fields. My hope is to spark curiosity and confidence in young women, showing them that this field is not only accessible but also incredibly rewarding.

Artificial Intelligence and AI Video

I also recently created a research video on Legal Information and AI in Canada. In this project, I explore the intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and the legal field, focusing on how AI technologies are transforming the accessibility and delivery of legal services in Canada. I examine how AI tools can streamline legal research, automate document review, and even assist in legal decision-making.

In this video, I share my insights on how AI can improve access to legal information, particularly for underserved communities, while also addressing the ethical and regulatory challenges it brings. I discuss both the opportunities and risks associated with AI’s growing influence in the Canadian legal landscape. My goal is to provide a deeper understanding of how AI is reshaping the future of law in Canada. Watch the video to learn more about my findings and perspectives on this exciting development.
