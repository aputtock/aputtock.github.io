Women in Air Treatment Scholarship

2022
Atlas Copco, Montréal Québec

The Women in Air Treatment Scholarship, offered by Atlas Copco, is designed to support and empower women pursuing careers in the field of air treatment and industrial engineering. This scholarship aims to address the gender imbalance in traditionally male-dominated industries like engineering, promoting greater diversity and inclusion. Atlas Copco, a global leader in industrial solutions, recognizes the importance of encouraging female talent to pursue technical careers, particularly in air treatment, which is crucial for various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare.

The scholarship provides financial assistance to women who are committed to pursuing higher education in relevant fields, such as mechanical or electrical engineering, with a focus on air treatment systems. In addition to the financial support, recipients are often offered mentorship opportunities and potential career pathways within Atlas Copco, further bridging the gap between education and professional success. This initiative reflects Atlas Copco's commitment to fostering innovation and sustainability through diverse and inclusive teams.
